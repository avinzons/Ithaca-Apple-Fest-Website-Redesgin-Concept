Food and Drink

With over 100 vendors present at AppleFest, there are many options of food that both young children and their parents will enjoy. This makes it convenient for these families to enjoy a meal at AppleFest, or to just sample or purchase some snacks. Here we have some of the most popular foods at AppleFest listed. Many other foods not listed here will be sold as well. This is just a brief overview.
